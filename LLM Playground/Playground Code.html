T
Temperature

Controls the randomness of the predictions. It scales the logits before Softmax.

Analogy:
Low (0.1): Like a conservative robot picking the most obvious word. Very focused, repetitive.

High (1.5+): Like a creative poet picking unusual words. More diverse, but prone to hallucinations.
K
Top K

Limits the next token selection to the K most probable tokens.

Analogy:
A buffet with 100 dishes. You tell the waiter, "Only bring me the top 3 most popular dishes." Everything else is ignored.
P
Top P (Nucleus)

Selects the smallest set of tokens whose cumulative probability exceeds P.

Analogy:
You want to be 90% sure you cover the good options. You keep picking the most likely dishes until the probability sum reaches 90%, then you cut off the rest.
